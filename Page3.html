ATEEZ the Rock Stars of K-Pop

Forging their own path

ATEEZ at the MAMA Awards on December 4th, 2019.

Top reasons why ATEEZ are unstopable:

  1. They're from a smaller agency which has allowed them to have close relationships with their management team. This has allowed them to have more creative control over their sounds and concepts
  2. Each member understand what their part is in the group and owns it.
  3. They have a unique sound that almost attacks you with how powerful it is.
  4. Their is a vibrant wildness to their sound and performances. Each member gives everything they've got, every since performance
  5. Their voices. Period.
  6. They cater to international audiences. They intentionally went after the international market by uploading all of their YouTube videos with English subtitles and going on an extensive World Tour soon after debuting.
  7. You know it, their music slaps.
