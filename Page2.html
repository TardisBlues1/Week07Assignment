Top reasons why Stray Kids is breaking records left and right:

  1. They're a self-made group that has control over the music they make. This allows them to create music that's meaningful to them allow them to perform with a special kind of passion
  2. Each member is an all-rounder meaning that they all sing, rap, and dance.
  3. Their is a true uniqueness to their sound.
  4. They're unparalled performers. Because of their immense talent and creativity, everything from their music videos to their live performances are bold, fresh, and eye catching.
  5. They are prolific. They release several high quality albums every year. In 2023, two of their albums sold over 4 million copies and were number one on the Billboard Top 100 chart upon release.
  6. And yes, their music slaps.
